Lab Research

  • Our vision is simple:

    Today’s Internet will evolve into the dominant network delivering all forms of data, voice, and video to anyone anywhere at anytime.

  • Our mission is also simple:

    The mission of WIRE Lab is to utilize Wireless Internet in our daily life. We focus on both theoretical Research and Engineering development so that novel ideas can be realized in real-world applications.
